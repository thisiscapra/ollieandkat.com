About us

We met over 6 years ago in London, where our passion for design cemented our relationship. We’ve travelled, moved to the country, got a dog, bought a house, and while we may already be married, a marriage without a wedding party with the ones you love around you is only half-way done. So, we picked our favourite spot in the world to host a great party!
